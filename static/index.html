    Welcome to FINEX Documentation

    FINEX is a next-generation AI financial analyst. It bridges the gap between raw financial data and actionable human-readable insights.

    This platform uses the AlphaVantage API for data and Google Gemini for AI analysis.

    Scoring Methodology

    We analyze three distinct pillars of a company's financial health:

    Profitability

    Measures efficiency in generating returns (ROE, Margins).

    Growth

    Evaluates Year-over-Year revenue and EPS increases.

    Safety

    Assesses liquidity and leverage risk (Debt-to-Equity).

    AI Verdicts

    Our integration with Google's Gemini LLM means the system actually "reads" the calculated metrics. It looks for correlations, identifies risks that mathematical models might miss, and issues a final Conviction Score.
